About EnglishPath

English Path is a web-based learning platform designed to help students learn English in an interactive, engaging, and structured way. This platform features a variety of educational tools, including learning materials, interactive quizzes, educational games, instructional videos, and progress tracking. With a user-friendly and visually appealing interface, English Path is suitable for learners at elementary to intermediate levels. All content is organized by level and theme, allowing users to study step by step according to their abilities.
