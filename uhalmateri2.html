Definition of Announcement

Announcements are typically used in schools, companies, public areas, or online platforms to deliver a message to a wide audience. They may be written or spoken, depending on the context and medium of communication.

Purpose of an Announcement

  • To inform the public or a group about an event or change.
  • To remind people about important dates or actions.
  • To invite people to participate in an activity or event.
  • To warn or give important instructions.

Body/Content

  • The main information: what, where, when, who, and why.
