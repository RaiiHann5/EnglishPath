Definition of Announcement

An Advertisement is a form of communication used to persuade or influence people to buy products, use services, or take action. In English, it is often shortened to ad.

Purpose of Advertisement

1. To Inform - Give information about a product or service.

2. To Persuade - Convince people to buy or try something.

3. To Remind - Make people remember a product or brand.

4.To build image - Create a positive image of a company or brand
